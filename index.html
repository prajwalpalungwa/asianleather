About Us

Asian Leather Industries produces quality leather goods with global export standards.

Asian Leather Industries was established by Mr. Zakir Hasan in 1989 with the objective of producing vegetable and chrome-finished leather from raw hides.

We specialize in finished leather made from cow and buffalo hides, catering to the footwear, furniture, and leather goods industries. Our two state-of-the-art factories, spread over 17,500 sqm, are equipped with modern machinery and have a combined annual production capacity of 10 million sqft of leather.

Recognized by government bodies as an Export House, Asian Leather Industries adheres to European standards and complies with REACH regulations. To maintain consistent quality, our products are free from all restricted chemicals outlined in the standards. Each batch is accompanied by a comprehensive physical and chemical testing report.

We operate a fully equipped in-house laboratory with advanced testing devices to carry out quality checks on every batch of leather.

Environmental sustainability is a core part of our operations. We have in-house water treatment and chrome recovery plants, allowing us to recycle waste and reuse water and chrome liquor in our production process.

At every step, we are committed to delivering high-quality leather through innovation, while ensuring minimal environmental impact.
